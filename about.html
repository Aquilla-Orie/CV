03 : About me

Who I am

Gabriel Chukwu is a game developer with limited professional experience but a zeal to learn and improve. His background in the .NET framework, algorithms, and gameplay programming inform his logical approach to problem solving. Gabriel is fueled by his passion for game development, which he strongly believes can be a very powerful tool for impacting lives. He considers himself as a 'life-long learner,' even though he has no intentions to build upon his academic foundation in Computer Science through continued coursework, but rather self learning and professional development.

Accomplishments

His hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has contributed greatly to both his personal life and his endeavours at every setting he has found himself.

  • In 2018, Gabriel became a memeber of Covenant University Developer Community(CDC) and it did not take long for him to be selected as Tech Lead for the Game Development Cluster in 2019.
  • 2020 was a very fulfilling year for Gabriel, he was priviledged to intern at The Sole Company as part of the Universiy Requirements. He did not take that opportunity lightly though, as he displayed an attitude of teamwork, attentiveness to detail and a zeal to learn. At the end of his stay September 2020, he was awarded Intern of the Year and earned the nickname 'Super Intern'.
  • Also in 2020, his drive for learning and self-development began to mainfest in his rankings on the Codewars platform, where he has climbed to the rank of 3kyu and is still going strong.
  • 2021, in his final year, Gabriel became the Co-Founder and Lead Developer of Covenant University Game Development Community, Covenant University's first fully independent game development community.
