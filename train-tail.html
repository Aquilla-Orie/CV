Train Tail

Platform: Android

Role: Programmer & Designer

Client: Mukesh Juadi

Developed as a client job, Train Tail is a simple passenger-pickup puzzle game where the player has to move the train along the tracks and pick up all the passengers while the train grows in size. In order to pass each level the player must avoid the train colliding with itself.

This game was originally published on the Google Play Store. However, due to changes in Google's policies and the requirement for regular updates to the Android SDK compatibility, the game was eventually taken down from the store. Despite this, the game remains fully functional and is available for download as an APK file below.
